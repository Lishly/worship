About God's Awe Worship Center

This ministry is called to share the Gospel of Jesus, disciple nation, raise a generation who shall worship the father in spirit and truth.

About Us

Gods awe worship center ministries was begun as a prayer fellowship in January 2020 by Rev. Jeremy and Tabitha Menda. By God's grace on September 27, 2020, it was established as a ministry based In Abilene Texas, United states of America. Since its inception, the ministry has continued to attract and minister to large numbers of people, locally and globally,both young and old in all walks of life. The name God's Awe Worship Center Ministries was prayerfully derived from Hebrews 12:28 "Therefore, let us be grateful for receiving a kingdom that cannot be shaken, and thus let us offer bo God acceptable worship, with reverence and Awe for our God is a consuming fire." The name reflects the main ministry and vision of the church. From that humble beginning, the ministry has grown and developed in diverse ways to the glory of God the father, his son Jesus Christ and the Holy Spirit, and the extension of his kingdom Amen.

Our Objectives

Our mission is to spread the Gospel, strengthen believers, and impact lives through worship, prayer, love, and service.

Worship in Spirit & Truth

Raising a generation that will worship God in spirit and truth with sincere devotion and commitment to His Word.

Teaching the Word

Equipping believers with Biblical truth for ministry, spiritual growth, and Christian service.

Prayer Warriors

Creating an army of prayer warriors across the globe dedicated to revival and intercession.

Fellowship & Love

Building a caring Christian community that demonstrates forgiveness, unity, compassion, and genuine love.

Serving the Needy

Caring for the poor, sick, and vulnerable through outreach, hospitality, and acts of kindness.

Faith & Perseverance

Encouraging believers to remain strong in faith despite challenges and difficult circumstances.
